DERIVATIVES DOCUMENTATION NEGOTIATOR TRAINING COURSE

Derivative Negotiator LLC offers two training courses in New York City for those interested in building their contract negotiating abilities:

Intro to ISDA Master Agreement Course

This is an immersive 4-hr ISDA bootcamp designed to give participants an intro into negotiating OTC derivatives documents.

March 17, 2018

$500

Advanced ISDA Master Agreement Course

This 3-hr course covers the more nuanced aspects of derivatives documentation and is geared toward current derivatives documentation ISDA negotiators interested in improving their capabilities.

March 31, 2018

$400

Both classes are capped at 10 attendees per session to allow for more intimate access to our highly experienced derivatives negotiator. The Intro and Advanced classes begin with an engaging lecture designed to incite various learning styles. It is followed by a small-group assignment, mock ISDA challenges, and a questions-and-answers session.

Please be advised that ISDA® documentation training courses provided by Derivative Negotiator LLC are not sponsored by or affiliated with the International Swaps and Derivatives Association, Inc (ISDA®).
